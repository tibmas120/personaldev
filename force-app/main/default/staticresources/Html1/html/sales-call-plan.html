Notes from Last Meeting

  • In our last meeting, what did we learn?
  • What opportunities did we identify?
  • What were our next steps? Did we do them?
  • What is our objective for the next meeting?
1000 Characters

Pre-Meeting: Plan

  • What is the meeting objective and our sales strategy? Overall account / meeting goals?
  • What will be top of mind for the customer?
  • What roles/people will be at the meeting? Is there a relationship strategy for each one?
  • Are there other IR team members who could enhance the sales call?
  • What insights, data, or stories can I share?
1000 Characters

Discover

  • What questions should I ask and how can I develop rapport and the relationship?
  • What info do I need to confirm or explore?
  • What focus words are important?
  • What are their expected “tough questions” and potential pitfalls? How should I handle these?
  • How can I ensure the meeting stays on track?
1000 Characters

Sell

  • What are the best areas to pursue?
  • What am I selling? What am I prepared to offer?
  • What are the expected objections and how will I handle them?
  • How will I be prepared to sell value before I decide to discount price?
  • How can I advance the sale in the pipeline?
  • What next step commitments do I want?
1000 Characters

Post-Meeting: Confirm

  • How did we do?
  • What did the customer and IR agree to do?
  • How well did that address the account goals? Meeting goals?
  • What progress was made in advancing a sale?
